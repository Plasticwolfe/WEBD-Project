About the Team:

Team Slumber Party is a group of three students currently attending Nova Scotia Community College: Center of Geographic Sciences (COGS) Campus in the IT-Programming Two-Year Course. The team are exactly what you'd expect from three IT students; Nerds, and loving every minute of it. While other teams might have skill, talent or even the smarts to get the job done; Team Slumber Party has a funny name, and a whole lot of heart.
